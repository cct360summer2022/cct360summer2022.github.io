OFFWHITE PERIOD

GRAY AREA BETWEEN BLACK AND WHITE AS THE COLOR OFF-WHITE

Abloh founded his first fashion house and second business overall in 2013 with the high-end streetwear brand Off-White, designing a logo inspired by Ben Kelly. Based in Milan, Italy, the company was described by Abloh as "the gray area between black and white as the color off-white" to investors and fashion critics.
LV PERIOD

FIRST AFRICAN DESCENT ARTISTIC DIRECTOR OF LV

Abloh was named artistic director of Louis Vuitton's menswear ready wear line, making him the first person of African descent to lead the brand's menswear line, as well as one of the few black designers at the helm of a major French fashion house.
